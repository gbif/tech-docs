= Processing of Taxonomic Identifiers in GBIF 


include::ROOT::partial$under-construction.adoc[] 

GBIF processes taxonomic identifiers that are provided on the DwC elements _scientificNameID_, _taxonID_ and _taxonConceptID_, when these identifiers belong to any of the identifier schemes available. The identifiers are retrieved from ChecklistBank and linked to taxa in the checklist. Currently, the schemes available are: 


[%autowidth.stretch]
|===
|Identifier scheme |Description |REGEX Format |Example

|https://artfakta.se/metadata/dyntaxa[Dyntaxa]
|A taxonomic database of the names of Swedish organisms, which contains information on current classification, recommended names and commonly used synonyms and misused names. 
|^(?:dyntaxa\|artfakta\|urn:lsid:dyntaxa.se:Taxon):(\\\d+)
|urn:lsid:dyntaxa.se:Taxon:106661

|https://www.ipni.org/[IPNI]
|The International Plant Names Index (IPNI) is a nomenclatural index of names of vascular plants published under the Code of Nomenclature for algae fungi and plants (ICN).
|^(?:ipni\|urn:lsid:ipni.org:names):(\\\d+-[1-3])
|urn:lsid:ipni.org:names:50959-2

|https://www.itis.gov/p[ITIS]
|The Integrated Taxonomic Information System, an authoritative taxonomic information on plants, animals, fungi, and microbes of North America and the world.
|^(?:itis\|tsn:)(\\\d+)
|tsn:80028

|https://www.nhm.ac.uk/our-science/data/uk-species/index[UK Species Inventory]: 
|A database of all UK wildlife, which provides the taxonomic foundation for most biological recording and analysis systems for the UK.
|^(?:uksi\|nhm):((?:NHMSYS\|NBNSYS)\\\d{10})
|nhm:NBNSYS0000161229

|https://www.marinespecies.org/index.php[WoRMs]: 
|The World Register of Marine Species (WoRMS) aims to provide an authoritative and comprehensive list of names of marine organisms, including information on synonymy.
|^(?:marinespecies\|worms\|aphia\|urn:lsid:marinespecies.org:taxname):(\\\d+)
|urn:lsid:marinespecies.org:taxname:538339

|===


When both an identifier field and a _scientificName_ are provided, the identifiers will be used first for matching, and if there is more than one identifier present, the information on _taxonID_ will take precedence. A match will also be made using the _scientificName_, and a comparison will be made between the two matches. 

https://techdocs.gbif.org/en/data-use/occurrence-issues-and-flags#taxonomic-issues[Taxonomic flags and issues] are assigned during https://techdocs.gbif.org/en/data-processing/taxonomy-interpretation#taxonomic-indexes[pipeline processing] if inconsistencies are detected in the applied identifiers.
